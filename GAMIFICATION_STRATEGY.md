## üö® Risk Mitigation

### 1. **Economic Balance**
- Set maximum discount limits (50% max)
- Cap coin redemption per transaction (30% max)
- Monitor total discount spend vs revenue
- Adjust conversion rates if needed

### 2. **Fraud Prevention**
- Limit referral rewards (max 10 per month)
- Validate booking completion before coins
- Block suspicious accounts
- Rate limit coupon creation

### 3. **User Burnout**
- Don't bombard with too many offers
- Keep UI clean, not cluttered
- Make rewards feel special, not spammy
- Focus on quality over quantity
